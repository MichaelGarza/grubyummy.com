Central Florida's guide to yummy grub...morning, noon or night!

About the Author

Hello. My name is Michael Garza and I am a front end web developer and food connoisseur. Combine the two and mix in some silly and presto!, GrubYummy is born.

A little bit of history about me which should answer the question: 'What qualifies you to review restaurants?'. I grew up in and around Austin, TX. and moved to Long Island, N.Y. at the age of 26. After 10 years in N.Y. my family and I relocated to Ocala, FL. In that time I was lucky enough to travel our great country for 2 years and eat some really good food along the way.

Featured Grub

Ok this pick may or may not be biased coming from a Texan...When I say come hungry I'm talking thanksgiving dinner hungry folks, the portions are huge from cat head sized bisquits and gravy to plate sized panckakes. Enjoy!

What to Expect

Food: I enjoy great tasting food from local burger joints to fine dining and do not discriminate based on cooking technique, fat content or plate appearance. If it's worth tasting I will try it and if it's really yummy you will see it somewhere on my site!

Atmosphere: At the end of the day the food is what it's all about but atmosphere can be very important to many of us. My favorite joints are typically local businesses with funky decor and always place emphasis on customer service, large portions and of course great tasting grub!

Price: I have found that you do not have to break the bank to find good food. Most of my picks will be average cost or under but make sure to check individual reviews for more accurate prices.
